üõí Orders & Shipping

How long does shipping take?

We offer three shipping options:

  • Standard Delivery: 2-3 business days (FREE)
  • Express Delivery: 1 business day (AED 15)
  • Same Day Delivery: Same day (AED 25)

Orders placed before 2 PM are processed the same day. Weekend orders are processed on Sunday.

What areas do you deliver to?

We deliver to all seven emirates in the UAE:

  • Dubai
  • Abu Dhabi
  • Sharjah
  • Ajman
  • Ras Al Khaimah
  • Fujairah
  • Umm Al Quwain

Free delivery is available for orders over AED 100. Remote areas may have additional delivery charges.

Can I track my order?

Yes! Once your order is shipped, you'll receive a tracking number via email and SMS. You can track your order status in real-time on our website or through our mobile app.

You'll receive updates at each stage: Order Confirmed ‚Üí Processing ‚Üí Shipped ‚Üí Out for Delivery ‚Üí Delivered.

What if I'm not home when delivery arrives?

Our delivery team will attempt delivery twice. If you're not available:

  • We'll leave a delivery note with contact information
  • You can reschedule delivery for a convenient time
  • Packages can be left with neighbors or building security (with your permission)
  • Items will be returned to our warehouse after 3 days

üå± Products

Are your products really eco-friendly?

Absolutely! All our products are carefully selected based on strict environmental criteria:

  • Made from sustainable, renewable materials
  • Biodegradable or recyclable packaging
  • No harmful chemicals or toxins
  • Certified by recognized environmental organizations
  • Support fair trade and ethical manufacturing

We provide detailed information about each product's environmental impact on our product pages.

How do I care for bamboo products?

Bamboo products are easy to care for:

  • Bamboo toothbrushes: Rinse after use, air dry, replace every 3-4 months
  • Bamboo cups: Hand wash with mild soap, avoid dishwasher
  • Bamboo utensils: Hand wash and dry immediately
  • General care: Keep dry when not in use, avoid soaking

Proper care ensures your bamboo products last longer and maintain their natural properties.

What materials are used in your products?

We use only sustainable, natural materials:

  • Bamboo: Fast-growing, naturally antimicrobial
  • Organic Cotton: Grown without pesticides or chemicals
  • Stainless Steel: Durable, recyclable, food-safe
  • Natural Fibers: Hemp, jute, and other plant-based materials
  • Plant-based Plastics: Made from renewable resources

All materials are sourced from certified suppliers who follow ethical and sustainable practices.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee:

  • Returns accepted within 30 days of delivery
  • Items must be unused and in original packaging
  • Free return shipping for defective items
  • Refunds processed within 5-7 business days
  • Exchange for different size/color available

Contact our customer service to initiate a return. We'll provide a return label and instructions.

How do I return an item?

Returning an item is easy:

  1. Contact customer service or use our online return form
  2. We'll send you a return label via email
  3. Package the item securely in original packaging
  4. Attach the return label and drop off at any authorized location
  5. Track your return status online

For defective items, we cover return shipping costs. For change of mind returns, return shipping is at customer's expense.

Can I exchange an item for a different size or color?

Yes! We offer exchanges for:

  • Different sizes (if available)
  • Different colors (if available)
  • Different products of equal or higher value

Exchange requests must be made within 30 days. You'll only pay the difference if exchanging for a higher-priced item. Contact customer service to arrange an exchange.
