About EcoHaven.ae

Leading the way in sustainable living across the UAE

Our Story

Founded in 2020, EcoHaven.ae emerged from a simple yet powerful vision: to make sustainable living accessible and affordable for everyone in the UAE. What started as a small initiative to reduce plastic waste has grown into the region's leading online marketplace for eco-friendly home products.

Our journey began when our founders noticed the increasing environmental challenges facing our planet and decided to take action. Today, we're proud to serve over 50,000 environmentally conscious customers across the UAE, helping them make sustainable choices without compromising on quality or convenience.
